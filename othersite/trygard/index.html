How to Use the Mouth Guard?

  1. Heat the water to about 80-90 degrees.
  2. Put the mouth guard in the water.
  3. Soak in water for about 30 seconds.
  4. Recommend: sipping water before putting in the mouthguard to moisten your mouth.
  5. Put the mouth guard into your mouth, adjust the guard to a comfortable position, bite the teeth, and squeeze the upper lip by hand to make the guard fit the teeth.
  6. Put in cold water for shaping.
